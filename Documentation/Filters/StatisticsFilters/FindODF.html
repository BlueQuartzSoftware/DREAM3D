Summary

Two sets of data are needed for this filter. These are Field{FieldEulerAngles, SurfaceFields, FieldPhases, Volumes} data and Ensemble{CrystalStructures} data. The filter finds the ODF.

Required Field Data

  • FieldEulerAngles
  • FieldPhases
  • SurfaceFields
  • Volumes

Required Ensemble Data

  • CrystalStructures
