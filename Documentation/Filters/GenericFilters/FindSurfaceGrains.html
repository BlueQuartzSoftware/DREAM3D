Summary

This filter determines which fields intersect the outer surface of the sample. This is accomplished by determining if any of the cells that are owned by the a field lie on the outer surface of the sample. If any one cell owned by a field lies on the outer surface of the sample, then that field is said to be a surface field.

Required Cell Data

  • GrainIds

Created Field Data

  • SurfaceFields
