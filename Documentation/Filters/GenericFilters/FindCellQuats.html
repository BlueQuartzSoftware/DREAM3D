Find Cell Quaternions Filter

Description | Options | Required Arrays | Created Arrays | Authors

Group

Generic Filters

Description

This filter determines the quaternions for each Cell, given the Euler angles for the Cell.
The quaternions calculated are rotated such that they all lie within the Fundamental Zone (FZ) for the crystal structure of the Cell's phase.
The FZ is the region of the crystal structure that contains general positions on which the symmetry operations of the structure cannot operate to generate other positions within the region that are symmetrically equivalent. The FZ is always bounded by mirror planes if present in the structure (cubic system). The FZ is also known as the Asymmetric Unit of the crystal structure.
