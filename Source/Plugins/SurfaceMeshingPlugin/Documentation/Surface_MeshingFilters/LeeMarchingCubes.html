Summary

This filter does creates a surface mesh using a Marching Cubes algorithm originally developed at Carnegie-Mellon University by Dr. Sukbin Lee in the Marterials Engineering department. Heavy modifications were performed by M. Grober and M. Jackson for the DREAM3D project.
