<h3>SyntheticBuilding Plugin</h3>

<p>This plugin contains filters that allow the construction of synthetic microstructures mainly representing polycrystal microstructures. Other microstructures can be generate with enough effort. Filters are capable of adding primary and secondary phases and matching the texture of the microstructure to an ODF</p>
