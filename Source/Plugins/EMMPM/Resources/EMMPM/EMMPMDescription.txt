<h3>EMMPM Plugin</h3>

<p>This plugin implements the Expectation Maximization/Maximization of Posterior Marginals algorithm that can segment gray scale images or other 8 bit unsigned data arrays that nominally form a <i>2D image</i>. The algorithm is well documented in the following paper:
</p>
<h4>Citations</h4>
<p>
H. C. Chuang, M. L. Comer, and J. Simmons, “Texture Segmentation in Microstructure Images of Advanced Materials,” In Proceedings of the <i>2008 IEEE Southwest Symposium on Image Analysis and Interpretation</i>, March 24-26, 2008, Santa Fe, NM
</p>
